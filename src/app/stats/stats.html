Percentages in the Total and Regular columns are relative to all cards opened. That is, if you opened 100 cards of which 70 are common then Total > Common would show (70%).
The Golden column shows percentages relative to the total of the same rarity. For example, if you opened 10 rares of which 2 are golden then Golden > Rare would show (20%).
The Extra columns (Regular Extra and Golden Extra) show how many cards you can freely disenchant because you have more of that card than you can put in a deck. In these columns the percentages are relative to the same rarity. That is, if you opened 3 golden epics of which 1 is extra, then Golden Extra > Epic would show (33.3%).
