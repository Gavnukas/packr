You can use similar search tags as in the Hearthstone client to filter your collection. Assuming that you are opening Gadgetzan packs you can search for:

  • Exact card name
    • searching for patches will show
  • Consecutive characters in the card name (fuzzy search)
    • searching for mom will show
    • searching for vp will show
  • Rarity
    • searching for rare will show all rare cards
    • searching for epic common will show all epic and common cards
    • searching for lgnd comn will show all legendary and common cards
  • Class
    • searching for mage will show all Mage cards
    • searching for kabal will show all Kabal cards
    • searching for hunter neutral will show all Hunter and Neutral cards
  • Mana
    • searching for mana:1 will show all OP one-drops
    • searching for mana:6+ will show all cards that cost 6 or more mana
    • searching for mana:1- will show all cards that cost 1 or less mana
    • searching for mana:3-4 will show all cards that cost 3 or more but 4 or less mana
    • searching for mana:2 mana:4 will show all cards that cost 2 or 4 mana
  • Owned
    • searching for owned:1 will show cards that you have only one of
    • the same ranges can be used as for mana
  • Extra
    • searching for extra will show legendaries that you have more than two of and other rarities that you have three of (including golden cards)
  • Missing
    • searching for missing will show legendaries that you have no copies of and other rarities that you have less than two of (including golden cards)
  • Golden
    • searching for golden will show cards that you have at least one golden copy of
  • Combinations
    • searching for golden epic will show epics that you have at least one golden copy of
    • searching for lgnd mana:2-5 will show legendaries that you have at 2 to 5 copies of
    • searching for mana comn will show
    • searching for owned:2 mana:3 a comn rare golden will show golden rare and common cards that cost 3 mana that you have exactly two of that also have an 'a' in their name
